Seattle Public Restroom Project

Who we are

We are a group of volunteers who are interested in gathering information on the locations of public bathrooms in Seattle are. We pride ourselves on presenting the most up-to-date information on the internet regarding the public restroom crisis.

Why we do it

Many children in the Greater Seattle Area have nowhere to go when it comes to using the bathroom in public. We have identified this as a rising problem in the King County area.

This is unacceptable and something needs to be done.
